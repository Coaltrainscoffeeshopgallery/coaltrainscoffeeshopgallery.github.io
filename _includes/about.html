About

Coaltrain's Coffee Shop Gallery is based at Barry Train Station located between Barry Docks and Barry Island train Stations. We offer a wide selection of hot and cold drinks including decaf and caffiene free options for early morning commuters and visitors who like to soak up the colourful and creative atmosphere curated by ownwers Al & Bri.

Alongside our extensive drinks and snacks menu that aims to offer something for everyone, we also offer a range of inclusive social events for folk to enjoy including live Jazz/Classical mornings, Album launches, Bad Poetry nights, Art Exhibitions, Ceramic Fairs and much more. Coaltrain's is a coffee shop that should be experienced.
