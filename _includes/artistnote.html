A Note from the Artist
Coaltrains coffee shop gallery has always carried a distinctive rhythm, a place where the aroma of morning coffee blends effortlessly with a spirit of creativity. Each visit brought not only warmth and flavour but also a quiet spark of inspiration. To be invited to design an illustrated poster capturing the essence of this space, along with refining their business cards, was both a joy and an honour. That collaboration has since grown into something even more meaningful: the opportunity to create an entire body of work for an exhibition opening this October. More will be shared soon, and I look forward to unveiling this new chapter with you.
